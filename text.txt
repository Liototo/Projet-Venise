This fanciful treatment oflberian history concerns the end ofthe Visigoths' reign but contrasts sharply
with David's text, set by Pollarolo as 1692/10. Roderic/Rodrigo, the last Gothic king to rule Spain,
seduced the young noblewoman Florinda with the promise of marriage. Upon the death of the ruler
Wittiza/Vitige, Roderic succeeded to the Spanish throne [71 0-711]. The future of Spain was thought
to depend upon the son to whom Florinda gave birth.285 The action took place in Seville.
